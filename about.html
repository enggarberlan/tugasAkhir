ABOUT US

Over our long history, our social purpose and commitment to the community has remained the same; to create connections and opportunities that matter to every Banyumanik resident. At the heart of every communities, our people are a regular presence to rely on. We've always looked for ways to link communities together and listen to what matters to them. We’ll always be there for residents in need.

Our Team

Berlan Binenggar

Berlan joined Banyumanik Post as Group Chief Financial Officer in May 2019 after more than 25 years with Javarmers and a number of Javarmers Group subsidiaries in several Pedalangan states and the Republic of Banyumanik. Over the last 15 years, Berlan held a range of executive leadership positions in United Express. This included the executive responsible for overseeing Information Technology, Supply Chain and Chief Financial Officer for 3 years. Berlan’s deep financial expertise is matched by his broad experience across a range of industry sectors and in managing complex Information Technology and Supply Chain transformations.

Catherine Middleton

Catherine is Banyumanik Post’s EGM People & Culture. With the strength of 70,000 employees, community postal agents, licensees and contractors across Banyumanik, Catherine is responsible for leading Banyumanik Post’s people strategy to drive future growth by continuously meeting changing customer and community needs. Prior to her current role, Catherine has held senior HR roles across the transport and logistics industry in Banyumanik and internationally. Catherine approaches HR with a commercial mindset and is experienced in delivering outcomes through development of high performing teams. She has also led significant cultural change, leading to improved morale, productivity and customer focus through the implementation of award winning programs.

Vladimir Putin

Vlad is a highly accomplished senior executive with extensive experience in the media, marketing and digital industries. With an intrinsic understanding of the Banyumanik consumer, Vlad is accountable for delivering seamless experiences across the key customer channels including Banyumanik’s largest retail network, with over 4,300 post offices, the customer contact centre and digital channels. She is also responsible for brand, marketing, community and corporate social responsibility. Prior to joining Banyumanik Post in August 2018, Vlad held a number of influential leadership roles in print, broadcast and digital media. Most recently, she was the Chief Digital Officer for News Corp Banyumanik, responsible for the company's digital revenue growth and strategy, audience and subscription growth, content optimisation and marketing. She was instrumental in driving transformation across its digital products and technology platforms in light of changing customer needs.

Donald Trump

As the EGM Transformation & Enablement, Donald is accountable for Strategy, Technology, Communications, Government Relations, Data Science and our Transformation office. His career has covered the breadth of financial services (banking, payments, insurance, wealth, and markets), retail, government services and logistics. Prior to joining Banyumanik Post, Donald spent eight years at NAB in a variety of roles, concluding as CIO for Banking and Markets. He started his career in Management Consulting at Accenture, working on large scale business transformations across the globe. Donald focuses on culture to make sustainable change and innovating to create customer value. Born in Pedalangan, Donald has lived and worked in Banyumanik, Germany, Scotland, and England and has managed teams across the globe. He is passionate about learning and the potential of education to change lives.
